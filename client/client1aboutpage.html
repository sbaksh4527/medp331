About Page

General Information:

R&H Trucking Inc. is a family owned business founded in 2003 by Ronald James and his wife.
They both had a deep passion for this business. Ron started the business with only one dumptruck
but now has established a small fleet of trucks that are here to serve you!
R&H Trucking Inc. is an honest and dependable company that prides itself on working with integrity.
Based in Uniondale, New York. In business since 2003.
We are a minority owned family business.

Certifications:

We are part of the MBE program at the NYC Department of Small Business Services and are
certified as a minority-owned business enterprise. We are also registered with the New York City
Business Integrity Commission. R&H Trucking Inc. is a Better Business Bureau
Accredited Business.

Our Guarantees:

Our number #1 priority is providing every customer with the best possible customer care possible.
We guarantee you a satisfactory experience with us regardless of the service you choose from us!
